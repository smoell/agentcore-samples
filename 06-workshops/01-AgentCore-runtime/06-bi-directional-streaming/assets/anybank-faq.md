## General Banking

### Q: How do I open an account with AnyBank?

**A:** You can open an account in three ways:

1. **Online** - Visit anybank.com and complete the application (takes about 10 minutes)
2. **Mobile App** - Download the AnyBank app and follow the guided setup
3. **In Branch** - Visit any of our 500+ branches with a valid ID

You'll need:
- Government-issued photo ID
- Social Security Number
- Proof of address (utility bill or lease)
- Initial deposit (varies by account type)

### Q: What's the difference between checking and savings accounts?

**A:** 

**Checking Accounts:**
- Designed for frequent transactions
- Unlimited deposits and withdrawals
- Comes with debit card and checks
- Lower or no interest
- Best for daily expenses

**Savings Accounts:**
- Designed for saving money
- Limited to 6 withdrawals per month
- Higher interest rates
- No checks or debit card
- Best for emergency funds and goals

### Q: Is my money safe at AnyBank?

**A:** Yes! All deposit accounts are FDIC insured up to $250,000 per depositor, per account ownership category. This means even if the bank fails, your money is protected by the federal government.

### Q: Can I have multiple accounts?

**A:** Absolutely! Many customers have:
- A checking account for daily expenses
- A savings account for emergency funds
- A high-yield savings for long-term goals
- Separate accounts for different purposes

There's no limit to the number of accounts you can have.

### Q: How do I close my account?

**A:** To close an account:

1. Ensure balance is zero
2. Stop all automatic payments and deposits
3. Wait for pending transactions to clear
4. Visit a branch, call us, or submit a secure message
5. Destroy any unused checks and debit cards

We'll send a final statement within 10 business days.

## Fees & Charges

### Q: How can I avoid monthly fees?

**A:** Each account has different requirements:

**Premium Checking:** Maintain $5,000 balance  
**Basic Checking:** Maintain $500 balance  
**High-Yield Savings:** Maintain $1,000 balance  
**Premium Savings:** No fees

Balance is calculated using average daily balance.

### Q: What happens if I overdraw my account?

**A:** If you spend more than your balance:

1. **With Overdraft Protection:**
   - We transfer from your linked savings ($10 fee)
   - Or use your overdraft line of credit (18% APR)
   - Transaction is approved

2. **Without Protection:**
   - Transaction is declined (no fee)
   - Or we may pay it and charge $35 overdraft fee
   - Maximum 3 overdraft fees per day

### Q: Are there fees for using ATMs?

**A:**

- **AnyBank ATMs:** Free
- **Other bank ATMs:** $3 per transaction
- **International ATMs:** $5 + 3% foreign transaction fee
- **Premium Checking:** All ATM fees waived

We have 50,000+ fee-free ATMs nationwide.

### Q: How much do wire transfers cost?

**A:**

**Domestic:**
- Outgoing: $25
- Incoming: $15

**International:**
- Outgoing: $45
- Incoming: $15

**Premium Checking:** All wire transfers are free

### Q: Can fees be refunded?

**A:** We may refund fees in certain situations:
- First-time overdraft (one-time courtesy)

Call customer service to request a review.

### Q: When will I receive my debit card?

### Q: What if my card is lost or stolen?

**A:**

- Premium card: No foreign transaction fees

### Q: How do I dispute a charge?

**A:**

1. Contact the merchant first (often fastest resolution)
2. If unresolved, contact us within 60 days:
   - Call customer service
   - Or submit dispute through online banking
3. We'll investigate (typically 10 business days)
4. Provisional credit issued if eligible
5. Final decision within 45 days

### Q: What's the difference between debit and credit cards?

**A:**

**Debit Card:**
- Uses money from your checking account
- No interest charges
- No credit check needed
- Helps control spending
- Limited rewards

**Credit Card:**
- Borrows money (pay back later)
- Interest charged on unpaid balance
- Requires credit check
- Builds credit history
- Better rewards and protections

## Mortgages

### Q: How much house can I afford?

**A:** General guidelines:
- Home price: 2.5-3x your annual income
- Monthly payment: 28% or less of gross income
- Total debt: 43% or less of gross income

**Example:**
- Income: $100,000/year
- Home price: $250,000-$300,000
- Monthly payment: $2,333 or less

Use our mortgage calculator at anybank.com for personalized estimates.

### Q: What's the difference between pre-qualification and pre-approval?

**A:**

**Pre-Qualification:**
- Quick estimate (15 minutes)
- Based on information you provide
- No credit check
- Not a commitment
- Good for initial planning

**Pre-Approval:**
- Detailed review (2-3 days)
- Requires documentation
- Credit check performed
- Conditional commitment
- Strengthens your offer

Always get pre-approved before house hunting!

### Q: How much do I need for a down payment?

**A:** Depends on loan type:

- **Conventional:** 3-20%
- **FHA:** 3.5%
- **VA:** 0% (for eligible veterans)
- **Jumbo:** 20%

**Benefits of 20% down:**
- No private mortgage insurance (PMI)
- Lower interest rate
- Lower monthly payment
- More equity from day one

### Q: What documents do I need for a mortgage?

**A:** Typical requirements:

**Income:**
- Last 2 years tax returns
- Last 2 months pay stubs
- W-2s for last 2 years
- Employment verification letter

**Assets:**
- Last 2 months bank statements
- Investment account statements
- Retirement account statements
- Gift letter (if using gift funds)

**Other:**
- Photo ID
- Social Security card
- Purchase agreement (when ready)

### Q: How long does mortgage approval take?

**A:** Timeline:

- **Pre-qualification:** 15 minutes
- **Pre-approval:** 2-3 business days
- **Full application:** 1 week
- **Underwriting:** 2-3 weeks
- **Closing:** 30-45 days from offer acceptance

**Total:** About 30-60 days from application to closing.

### Q: Can I pay off my mortgage early?

**A:** Yes! All our mortgages have:
- No prepayment penalties
- No fees for extra payments
- Flexible payment options

Benefits of paying early:
- Save thousands in interest
- Build equity faster
- Own your home sooner
- Peace of mind

## Security & Fraud

### Q: How do I protect myself from fraud?

**A:** Best practices:

**Online:**
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable multi-factor authentication
- Never share passwords or PINs
- Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking
- Keep software updated

**Phone:**
- Never give account info over phone
- We'll never ask for your full password
- Verify caller identity
- Hang up and call us back if suspicious

**Email:**
- We'll never ask for passwords via email
- Don't click suspicious links
- Verify sender address
- Report phishing to fraud@anybank.com

### Q: What if I notice suspicious activity?

**Response time:** We respond to fraud reports within 1 hour.

## Account Access

### Q: Can someone else access my account?

**A:** Only with proper authorization:

**Joint Account Holder:**
- Full access to account
- Can make transactions
- Receives statements
- Equal ownership

**Authorized User:**
- Limited access (you define)
- Can make transactions
- No ownership rights
- Can be removed anytime

**Power of Attorney:**
- Legal document required
- Access as specified in POA
- Must be notarized
- Reviewed by our legal team

### Q: What happens to my account if I die?

**A:**

**Individual Account:**
- Becomes part of estate
- Frozen until probate
- Distributed per will or state law

**Joint Account:**
- Passes to surviving owner
- No probate needed
- Immediate access

**Payable on Death (POD):**
- Passes to named beneficiary
- No probate needed
- Beneficiary provides death certificate

We recommend consulting an estate planning attorney.

### Q: Can I access my account from abroad?

**A:** Yes! But take precautions:

**Before Travel:**
- Set travel notification
- Enable international access
- Save customer service numbers
- Download mobile app
- Note time zone differences

**While Abroad:**
- Use secure internet only
- Avoid public computers
- Monitor account regularly
- Keep phone charged
- Have backup access method

**Fees:**
- International ATM: $5 + 3% fee
- Foreign transactions: 3% (waived for Premium)
- Wire transfers: $45 outgoing
